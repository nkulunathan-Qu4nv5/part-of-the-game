Build a football fan content generator app called 
"Part of the Game". 

Users enter:
- Match result (home team, away team, score)
- Key moments from the match
- Their favorite team or player
- The type of content they want: 
  Social Post, Hot Take, Meme Caption, 
  Match Reaction, or Story for a Friend
- Their fan perspective: won, lost, drew, or neutral
- Their tone: confident, funny, savage, or heartbroken

The app generates personalized fan content 
that sounds like a real football fan — 
not a journalist. It also generates a 
matching visual in an Adidas-inspired 
sport editorial style.
